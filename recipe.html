Quarter Pound Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 lb(s) (500 g) lean ground beef
  • 1 cup (250 ml) All-Bran Original* cereal
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup (50 ml) finely chopped onion
  • 2 Tbsp (30 ml) ketchup
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp (2 ml) salt
  • ¼ tsp (1 ml) pepper
  • ⅒ tsp (0.5 ml) garlic powder
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) cooking oil

Steps:

  1. In large bowl, combine all ingredients, except oil. Mix well and shape into 4 uniform patties.
  2. In large non-stick skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add patties; cook 3 to 4 minutes per side. Alternatively, grill over medium-high heat on barbecue or under broiler.
  3. Serve on toasted whole grain rolls with your favourite condiments.

Tip:

  • Burgers will hold together better if chilled before cooking.
  • VARIATION: Add 2 ml (1/2 tsp) each: dried basil and dried oregano. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  • VARIATION - Add 125 g (4 oz) old Cheddar cheese cut into tiny cubes
